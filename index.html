Recycling Alert!

During times of unsafe road conditions, you may experience service delays or disruptions. Our top priority is the safety and well-being of our team members and the public. SSC is committed to providing service to the best of our ability in areas that can be safely accessed. If we cannot collect material on your regular service day due to unsafe road conditions, please see the schedule for your next collection. We will take twice the usual garbage or FoodPlus! compost at no extra charge.

What Happens to Recyclables?

As the waste collectors cart your recyclables away, you’re suddenly hit by some terrible questions: Where do those materials go once they're picked up? Will they will actually be recycled? How do you know they’re not just going to end up in a landfill? In Whatcom County, our recyclables go to a good home.
